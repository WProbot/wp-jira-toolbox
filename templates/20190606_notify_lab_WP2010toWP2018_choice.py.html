Dear Sir or Madam,

EPFL Web sites are changing their graphic charter. The website {{ WordPress_url }} you are responsible for, does not show yet the new visual identity.
To make the change, we propose two options:

  • copy your current site and apply the graphic charter in a preparation environment {{ QA18_url }}. It will be necessary to control the pages and to adapt them if necessary.
  • start from a blank site we will create in a preparation environment {{ QA18_url }}. You will then create your new content.

To organize your migration, return your choice by sending us an email wp-migration@epfl.ch (empty site or copy).

Some info

  • During this preparation phase, your site {{ WordPress_url }} (old charter) remains the one visible to the public.
  • at the end of your adaptation we will copy {{ QA18_url }} at the following location {{ url_ventilee }} instead of {{ WordPress_url }}. If you need to publish some content during the preparation, think about adding it on both sites, the old one and the one you are finalizing. It is therefore recommended to minimize the preparation time to avoid double work.
  • When finished, you will have to come back to us to put your new pages online, archiving the old pages and redirecting to the new site.


Tips : Help and information are available at https://help-wordpress.epfl.ch - You have the possibility to work in groups at the work and share sessions permanences WEB - If you are outside the EPFL network, you need the VPN.
